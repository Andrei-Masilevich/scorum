Detailed Description

Setup a versting withdraw to another account.

Allows an account to setup a vesting withdraw but with the additional request for the funds to be transferred directly to another account's balance rather than the withdrawing account. In addition, those funds can be immediately vested again, circumventing the conversion from scorum power to scorum and back, guaranteeing they maintain their value.
